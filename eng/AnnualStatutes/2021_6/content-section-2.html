Marginal note:Definitions

  • 2 (1) The following definitions apply in this Act.

    collective agreement

    collective agreement means the collective agreement between the employers’ association and the union that expired on December 31, 2018. (convention collective)

    employee

    employee means a person who is employed by an employer and bound by the collective agreement. (employé)

    employer

    employer means the employers’ association or any of its members. (employeur)

    employers’ association

    employers’ association means the Maritime Employers Association. (association patronale)

    mediator-arbitrator

    mediator-arbitrator means the mediator-arbitrator appointed under subsection 8(2). (médiateur-arbitre)

    Minister

    Minister means the Minister of Labour. (ministre)

    union

    union means the Syndicat des débardeurs — Canadian Union of Public Employees Local 375. (syndicat)

  • Marginal note:Words and expressions

    (2) Unless otherwise provided, words and expressions used in this Act have the same meaning as in Part I of the Canada Labour Code.

  • Marginal note:Presumption

    (3) For the purposes of this Act, the union is deemed to be a person.
